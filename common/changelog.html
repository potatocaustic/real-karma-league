Changelog

Recent updates and improvements to the RKL website.

July 16, 2025

  • New
    Regular Season Awards: Three regular season awards (best team, best performances) have been potsed to the trophy case page. Congrats to the winners!

July 5, 2025

  • Improved
    Team Page Optimization: Streamlined client-side data processes to help the team profile pages load faster.

July 3, 2025

  • Improved
    WAR Formula Update: The definition of a replacement-level performance was changed from 80% of the leaguewide median to 90%, better reflecting the 180th-best REL median value in the league. (This should not be changed again; 90% appears to be an accurate definition of replacement-level using prior seasons' data, too.)

July 2, 2025

  • New
    Playoff Bracket: Added a playoff bracket that is accessible through the standings & rankings page. Links to the bracket will be added elsewhere once the postseason begins.

June 21, 2025

  • New
    Comparison Tool: Added a new Comparison Tool for users to see how any two players or teams stack up against each other! Team comparisons also feature a couple new stats that are described in the advanced stats glossary.
  • New
    Trophy Case: Added a new Trophy Case page to display league champions, award winners, and All-Stars.
  • Improved
    Navigation: Replaced the "Leaderboards" link with a new "Stats Hub" dropdown menu, which now contains links to Leaderboards and the new Comparison Tool for a more organized layout.

June 15, 2025

  • New
    Dark Mode: Implemented a site-wide dark mode theme toggle. Click on the sun/moon icon (top right on desktop; top left on mobile) to switch themes. Your preference is saved locally for future visits.

June 14, 2025

  • New
    All-Star Week: The all-star, rising stars, and GM games have been added to the league schedule and will be assigned dates when available.
  • New
    Stats Glossary: Added an advanced stats glossary for users to learn about metrics such as REL, GEM, and WAR.
  • Improved
    Navigation: Consolidated draft-related pages into a "Draft Central" dropdown menu for a cleaner header.
  • New
    S8 Draft Lottery Page: Created a Tankathon-style lottery page and simulator for the upcoming S8 draft.
  • New
    S7 Draft Results: Added season 7 draft results with corresponding player stats. Also added an "R" badge to identify rookies on leaderboards and team rosters.

June 13, 2025

  • New
    Added GEM and WAR leaderboards: Added player leaderboards for GEM (geometric mean player rank) and WAR (Wins Above Replacement - new!). These two stats also make an appearance on player/team profiles.
  • New
    Added Changelog: Created page to document site improvements and edits.
